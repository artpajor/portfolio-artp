Art Pajor

Full Stack Software Developer

Hi! My name is Art and a few months ago, I left my job and started a 16 – week coding bootcamp at iO Academy in Bath, UK. I did it because my ultimate personal goal is expansion – on many levels. My previous career choices, some made from a desire to make a difference, some from necessity, left the area of my mental expansion unattended and I knew this had to change. I am a person who is very focused on other people and I am always thinking – how can I improve the world around me? What can I do for humanity on a bigger scale with the tools I’ve got? Where can I find bigger tools? So, when I discovered coding, I thought - “Finally! This is the tool I love and need in my life!”. I love the challenge and satisfaction that comes with building things. I love the feeling of putting the puzzle together and I cannot wait to combine that passion with an opportunity to learn how to build impactful software. Being allowed to grow and learn continuously, and having a chance to bring some positive change, is how I thrive.
