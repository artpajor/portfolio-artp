My path towards software development wasn't straightforward. As a female bodied person I never thought that the tech industry is accessible for me, nor that coding would be an easy skill to learn. Luckily, I am a challenge seeker! In my previous career choices I lacked either mental stimulation or career growth potential, which led me to a crossroad and a question - what to do next? After doing some research and trying numerous short coding courses available on - line, I already knew that this career path could provide me with what I was looking for - an endless potential for learning and growth. I thoroughly love what I've learned at iO Academy and I couldn't be more excited about using my newly obtained knowledge to make a difference, on a larger scale and more personal level. Integrity is very important to me and I would love to dedicate my efforts to a company with strong ethics and an emphasis on creating an inclusive, respectful and community focused workplace.
